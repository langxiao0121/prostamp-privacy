Overview

ProStamp ("we", "our", or "the app") is a photo watermarking application designed for field service documentation. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS app.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Photos or images you take
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data on external servers

Camera Permission

ProStamp requests access to your device's camera solely to capture photos for watermarking. Photos are processed entirely on your device and are saved to your local photo library. We never access, upload, or transmit your photos.

Location Permission

ProStamp requests access to your device's location to embed GPS coordinates and address information as a watermark on your photos. Location data is used only at the moment of capture to generate the watermark text. It is never stored by the app, transmitted to any server, or shared with any third party.

Photo Library Permission

ProStamp saves watermarked photos directly to your device's photo library. We do not access existing photos in your library beyond what is necessary to complete your requested action.

Data Storage

All app data, including templates and settings, is stored locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers or cloud services operated by us.

Third-Party Services

ProStamp does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

ProStamp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
